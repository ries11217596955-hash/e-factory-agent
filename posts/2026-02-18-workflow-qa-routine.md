## What this solves

After deployments or batch updates, things silently break:
- pages disappear
- links fail
- content breaks

## When to use

- after deploy
- after batch updates
- before traffic

## Steps

1. Open key pages  
2. Click navigation  
3. Test full path  
4. Check content  
5. Quick load check  

## Common mistakes

- checking only build
- not clicking links
- ignoring content

## Result

Stable site, fewer failures
