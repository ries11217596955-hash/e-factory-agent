## Goal

Increase reviews and trust.

## Workflow

1. Detect delivery  
2. Wait 7–14 days  
3. Send simple request  
4. Filter unhappy users  

## Mistakes

- too early
- too complex
- ignoring feedback

## Result

More reviews, better ratings
