# Outcome
A pre-season checklist that reduces rushed listing edits.

Hub: [Amazon AI](/hubs/amazon-ai/)

## Refresh areas
- title and bullets
- seasonal modifiers in the keyword map
- main image and supporting visuals
- A+ emphasis blocks
- review and Q&A issues that must be resolved first
- pricing and promo timing

## Workflow
1. Start the review 3-6 weeks before the seasonal window.
2. Re-check keyword clusters and competitor gaps.
3. Update one positioning angle, not the whole story.
4. Refresh imagery only if it supports the same angle.
5. Review pricing and promo plan together with the listing change.
6. Save one dated refresh note for the next season.

## Why it works
Seasonal wins often come from disciplined preparation, not last-minute creative changes.

## Common mistakes
- refreshing everything at once without a clear angle
- changing copy after traffic spikes instead of before
- forgetting to document what was tested this season
