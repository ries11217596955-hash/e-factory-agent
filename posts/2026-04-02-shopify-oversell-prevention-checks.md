# Shopify oversell prevention checks

Overselling usually starts with silent mismatch, not with one dramatic bug.

## Check these first

- delayed stock sync
- bundle math
- manual edits
- app conflict
- reserve stock rules

## Minimum discipline

Use one source of truth for quantity changes and log every exception.
