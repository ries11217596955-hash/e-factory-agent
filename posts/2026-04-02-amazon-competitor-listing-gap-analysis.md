# Outcome
A short gap sheet that tells you what to refresh next.

Hub: [Amazon AI](/hubs/amazon-ai/)

## Compare these areas
- title clarity
- bullet structure
- image angle and promise
- A+ positioning
- review signal themes
- pricing / promo pressure

## Workflow
1. Pick 3-5 close competitors, not giant category leaders from a different tier.
2. Capture one screenshot or copy snapshot for each listing area.
3. Score each area as:
   - stronger than us
   - roughly equal
   - weaker than us
4. Write one sentence per gap:
   - what they do better
   - why it matters
   - which asset you would change first
5. Convert the top 3 gaps into one refresh cycle.

## Why it works
A useful competitor review reduces vague anxiety. You stop copying everything and focus on the few differences that affect discoverability or conversion.

## Common mistakes
- comparing against the wrong product tier
- turning the gap sheet into a giant spreadsheet nobody uses
- changing price before checking offer and positioning gaps
