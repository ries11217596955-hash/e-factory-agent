# Shopify product page friction checklist

Use this checklist when traffic exists but product pages under-convert.

## Review these areas

- title clarity
- benefit-first copy
- image sequence
- shipping and return trust
- variant confusion
- weak FAQs

## Typical blockers

- unclear fit or sizing
- missing comparison information
- weak proof
- hidden delivery timing

## Fast action

Fix the top 3 blockers first, then recheck conversion before rewriting the whole page.
