# Shopify support ticket intent router

Not every support ticket needs the same response lane.

## Core intents

- order status
- return or refund
- product question
- payment issue
- damaged item
- missing delivery

## Routing rule

The goal is simple:
- urgent and costly tickets first
- repetitive tickets into templates
- ambiguous tickets into review
