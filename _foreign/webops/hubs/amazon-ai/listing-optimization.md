## What matters here

Amazon listing optimization is not "write prettier copy."  
It is conversion work under platform constraints:

- clearer relevance
- cleaner structure
- fewer compliance mistakes
- faster iteration without turning the listing into AI sludge

## Practical sequence

1. collect product facts and constraints
2. cluster keywords by buyer intent
3. draft titles and bullets with AI
4. align images and A+ blocks with the same positioning
5. run a QA pass for claims, clarity, and duplication
6. publish only the cleaned version
7. monitor conversion and listing health
