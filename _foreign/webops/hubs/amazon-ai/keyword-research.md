## What matters here

Keyword research for Amazon is not a giant export of phrases.  
It is a usable map that supports listing decisions:

- core intent
- modifiers
- problem language
- backend leftovers

## Practical sequence

1. collect raw terms from current listing, competitors, and customer language
2. group by intent and product angle
3. assign high-priority terms to title, bullets, image text plan, and backend
4. remove duplicates and low-signal clutter
5. keep one simple keyword map for future refreshes
