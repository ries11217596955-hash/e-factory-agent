## What matters here

Pricing work on Amazon is not constant repricing.  
It is signal handling:

- detect meaningful changes
- separate one-off noise from sustained pressure
- route action back to listing, promo, or margin review

## Practical sequence

1. track price and promo changes for your ASIN and close competitors
2. ignore single-day noise unless it affects conversion or Buy Box pressure
3. review whether the problem is price, positioning, or offer quality
4. decide on one action and log the date
5. re-check after the next signal window
