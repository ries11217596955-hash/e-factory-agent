# Shopify product Q&A extraction workflow

Customer questions reveal where the product page is weak.

## Workflow

1. collect repeated questions from chat, email, and tickets
2. cluster them by product or objection
3. write short reusable answers
4. add the best answers to product pages and FAQ blocks

## Why it works

Questions show:
- uncertainty
- trust gaps
- fit concerns
- missing comparisons
