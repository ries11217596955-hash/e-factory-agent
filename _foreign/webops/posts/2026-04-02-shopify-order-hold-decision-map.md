# Shopify order hold decision map

Some orders should pause before the team moves forward.

## Typical hold reasons

- fraud risk
- address mismatch
- stock conflict
- product customization question
- duplicate order

## Output

For each hold reason define:
- who reviews it
- what evidence is required
- what action clears the order
