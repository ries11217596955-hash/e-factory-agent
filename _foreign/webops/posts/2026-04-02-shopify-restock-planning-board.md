# Shopify restock planning board

A restock board makes inventory decisions visible.

## Prioritize by

- stockout risk
- revenue impact
- seasonality
- supplier delay risk

## Board columns

- watch
- order now
- confirm supplier
- delayed
- replace or bundle alternative
