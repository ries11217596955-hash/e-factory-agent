# Shopify low-stock alert ladder

Low stock should trigger action before it becomes a customer issue.

## Alert ladder

- watch
- warning
- urgent
- stop-sell

## Good inputs

- sales velocity
- supplier lead time
- open purchase orders
- bundle dependency
